Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-14

This Privacy Policy explains how DotShot (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use our iOS game.

What we collect

  • App diagnostics (crash reports, performance, device model, OS version) to keep the app stable.
  • Usage analytics (feature usage, session counts) to improve gameplay. We use PostHog with privacy options enabled where possible (IP anonymization, no cross‑app tracking).
  • Purchase information (product IDs, timestamps, receipts) to deliver in‑app purchases. Processing is via Apple and RevenueCat.

What we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, or phone number in‑app.
  • No precise location.
  • No advertising identifiers for third‑party ad tracking.

How we use data

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the App.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraud.
  • Provide support when you contact us.

Sharing

We share limited data with service providers that help us run the App, such as Apple (App Store), RevenueCat (purchase validation), and PostHog (product analytics). These providers process data on our behalf under their terms.

Your choices

  • You can disable analytics in the App settings (if provided) or your device’s privacy settings.
  • You can request deletion of analytics identifiers by emailing support@dotshot.app.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided data, contact us and we’ll delete it.

International transfers

We may process data on servers outside your country. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information.

Contact

Questions? support@dotshot.app

This plain‑language policy is for convenience and not legal advice.
