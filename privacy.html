Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 14, 2025

DotShot ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Automatically collected data: device type, operating system, crash data, app usage statistics.
  • In-app purchase data: transaction details processed through Apple.
  • No personal identifiers: we do not collect your name, email, or address directly.

2. Use of Information

We use this information to:

  • Improve game performance and fix bugs.
  • Analyze gameplay for balancing and user experience.
  • Process in-app purchases through Apple.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics and crash reporting providers (such as Apple, Google, or others) to collect and process information under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Transfers

Your information may be stored or processed on servers located outside of your country, including the United States.

5. Data Retention

We retain crash logs and analytics data only as long as necessary to improve our services, unless required by law to keep them longer.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU, UK, California), you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Request a copy of the data we have.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: hello@purrfectly.app. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 where required by law). If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us for removal.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Effective Date" at the top when changes are made.

10. Contact Us
