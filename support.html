FAQ

How do I restore purchases?

On iOS, open DotShot → Settings → “Restore Purchases”. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID used to buy items.

The game feels slow or laggy.

Try closing background apps and ensuring Low Power Mode is off. If issues persist, contact support with your device model and iOS version.
